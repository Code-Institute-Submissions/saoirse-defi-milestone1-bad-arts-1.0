Same D4Eance


Known as the "Nate Dogg" of Limerick City. He is 1/3 of Same D4Ence, a Limerick group consisting of SiZe, MCB and PX music's Hazey Haze. They all originate from the famed Island Field, Limerick City and have grown up together since. Some previous projects that SiZe has been a part of include - Same D4Ence: Basic Instinct, Exclamation Marks, Affiliation Part 1 and the most recent release, SiZe & MCB: Flood Ground. He has worked with a variety of renowned producers including Naive Ted, Mynameisjohn, Mankyy,BigMan Davey and DJ Green. SiZe has opened up for acts such as Ghostface Killah, Levelz & Beardyman and gigged at Electric Picnic, The Scratch Games, Dolans and More!
