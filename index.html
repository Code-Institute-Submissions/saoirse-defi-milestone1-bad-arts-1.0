SiZe

Latest video release:
Bio:

Is the original founder of Bad Arts Entertainment. He is 1/3 of Same D4Ence, a Limerick group consisting of SiZe, MCB and PX music's Hazey Haze. They all originate from the famed Island Field, Limerick City. Some previous projects that SiZe has been a part of include - Same D4Ence: Basic Instinct, Exclamation Marks, Affiliation Part 1 and the most recent release, SiZe & MCB: Flood Ground. He has worked with a variety of renowned producers including Naive Ted, Mynameisjohn, Mankyy,BigMan Davey and DJ Green. SiZe has opened up for acts such as Ghostface Killah and gigged at Electric Picnic, The Scratch Games, Dolans and More!

A10Sin

Is an emcee originally from Galway, having lived in Limerick for the last couple of years. He is 1/3 of the group Field Literature which includes himself, DJ Green and Trust. His previous full releases: The Crettin LP (2019), The Common Rough LP (2020), The Devil Was A Culchie (Field Literature, 2020) and Son Of Shylin E.P x Shy Son (2020). He has also released two mixtapes called "Deficit Disorder" (2019) and "Ladies & Gentlemen" (2020) which were both a compilation of unreleased tracks and features. A10Sín prefers the recluse of his studio and has yet to perform a live gig under his current emcee persona!

DJ Green

Is a Galway native artist similar to A10Sín and has been producing since 2014. He is a member of the group Field Literature. Some of his production credits include: Kneecap, Stevo Timothy, Strange Boy, Same D, Wu-Tang Killa Beez, Project Pat, Jack Bashful, Upfront, Trust, Bigman Davey. His previous full releases include Sick Caint x Animal (2018), Kushlawn Cocá (2019) and San An Fiain (2020). He has been involved with the running of gigs including Breaking Down Borders 1 + 2 as well as a variety of other gigs held in Pharmacia and Costello's Milk Market. He has Djed for a long list of artists including Costello, Lazy Eyez, Upfront and so many more from all four corners of the country.
