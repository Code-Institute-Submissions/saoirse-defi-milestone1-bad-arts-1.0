SiZe

Latest video release:
Bio:

Is the original founder of Bad Arts Entertainment. He is 1/3 of Same D4Ence, a
Limerick group consisting of SiZe, MCB and PX music's Hazey Haze. They all originate from the famed Island Field,
Limerick City. Some previous projects that SiZe has been a part of include - Same D4Ence: Basic Instinct, Exclamation Marks, Affiliation Part 1 and the most recent release, SiZe & MCB: Flood Ground. He has worked with a variety of renowned producers including Naive Ted, Mynameisjohn, Mankyy,BigMan Davey and DJ Green. SiZe has opened up for acts such as Ghostface Killah and gigged at Electric Picnic, The Scratch Games, Dolans and More!

A10Sin

Latest video release:

Is an emcee originally from Galway, having lived in
Limerick for the last couple of years. He is 1/3 of the group Field Literature which includes himself, DJ Green and Trust. His previous full releases: The Crettin LP (2019), The Common Rough LP (2020), The Devil Was A Culchie (Field Literature, 2020) and Son Of Shylin E.P x Shy Son (2020). He has also released two mixtapes called "Deficit Disorder" (2019) and "Ladies & Gentlemen" (2020) which were both a compilation of unreleased tracks and features. A10Sín prefers the recluse of his studio and has yet to perform a live gig under his current emcee persona!

Trust

Trust drew inspiration from Lunitic and working class records, much like A10Sín, which lead him to delve deep the down the path of the Irish and their history of poetry. He linked up with Steo Skitz, whom in his own words is the first person to believe in his abilities. They joined together for the album “Stranger Than Fiction” which is similar to the Field Literature music in regards to lyrical content, style and themes. Somewhere down the line he linked up with DJ Green and A10Sín to form Field Literature and they released their first album "The Devil Was A Cuclhie" in 2020.
