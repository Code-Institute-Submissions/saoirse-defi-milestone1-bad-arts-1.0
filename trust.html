Trust

Trust drew inspiration from Lunitic and working class records, much like A10Sín, which lead him to delve deep the down the path of the Irish and their history of poetry. He linked up with Steo Skitz, whom in his own words is the first person to believe in his abilities. They joined together for the album “Stranger Than Fiction” which is similar to the Field Literature music in regards to lyrical content, style and themes. Somewhere down the line he linked up with DJ Green and A10Sín to form Field Literature and they released their first album "The Devil Was A Cuclhie" in 2020.
